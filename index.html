MY JOURNEY

I started screaming my lungs out at a very young age, around 14, absolutely destroying my voice and sounding like crap for a while.

After a couple of years of relentless practice, I finally found my voice and embraced techniques that allowed me to unleash my full potential.

After quite some touring and performing, I gained invaluable experience that shaped my style.

My journey has been filled with challenges, but each performance has only fueled my passion for metal music.

Now, I want to share my knowledge and help aspiring vocalists unleash their true potential.
